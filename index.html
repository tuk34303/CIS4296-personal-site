Car Rental Service Project

In my component-based software design class, one of our projects we did was a car rental service where we had a many different types of classes such as a car class that would allow input for a cars information such as the make, model, year and so forth. We would also have a customer class which would take in the customers info like their name, phone number and so forth, and many more types of classes such as a rental class that would deal with the rental trancations and availabilty regarding whether or not a certain car was available. This project was one of my better well built projects as well as one of my favorites due to my interest in cars, and due to the amount of work that was put into this project as well considering the car rental service project was one of our bigger projects in the class.
